Microsoft DLP helps prevent accidental data leaks by detecting sensitive info like SSNs, credit cards, and health records across emails, files, and messages.

Capabilities:
- Policy templates for HIPAA, GDPR, PCI, etc.
- Detects sensitive data in Outlook, Teams, SharePoint, OneDrive.
- Auto-block or notify based on severity.
- Integrates with Microsoft Purview for advanced insights.
- Allows user justifications and overrides.
- Admins get detailed incident reports.

Use cases:
- Prevent employee from sharing SSNs via email
- Block uploading credit card numbers to public SharePoint
- Warn HR when health records are attached to chats
